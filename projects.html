Festive-O-Meter
http://festive.site
A holiday-themed hack for HackTheHolidays, working out how festive you are from your tweets.
Working together with Peran Truscott, Matt Burman and Pandelis Zembashis, we created something that calculated how festive a user was based on the language used in their tweets. We created a JS sentiment engine and a node.js server, as well as a MongoDB database. We came 2nd overall, and also won the prize for best domain name (https://festive.site) After speaking to a developer at Twitter, we were able to get the API rate limits lifted over the Christmas period, and we pushed out a further-developed app, after putting a couple of day's extra work into the project.
GeoDonation
https://github.com/bedekelly/geodonation
A hack built at Manchester's GreatUniHack 2015. It aimed to solve the the problem that charities face of location-based analysis -- where most of their donations come from, and how they're distributed.
